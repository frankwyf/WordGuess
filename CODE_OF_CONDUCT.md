# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating an open, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical issues and project goals.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing, threats, or intimidation.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after moderation feedback.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors for behavior that violates this policy.

## Reporting

Please report serious issues by opening a private security advisory or contacting maintainers through repository channels.
